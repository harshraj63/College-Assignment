5. Sabhyata Dwar (Civilization Gate)

Established :- 20 May 2018

Stays open:- 10:00 AM - 05:00 PM
Stays closed:- Monday

Sabhyata Dwar is a sandstone arch monument located on the banks of the Ganges River in the city of Patna. The Civilization Gate is designed with Mauryan-style architecture with the aim of showing the ancient glory of Pataliputra and traditions and culture of the state of Bihar. The civilization gate has been constructed by importing red and white sand stone from Rajasthan. The building construction department has built a beautiful garden around the civilization gate. The garden is also attracting tourists to its side. Gateway of India in Mumbai, India Gate in New Delhi and Fatehpur SikriIt is also the gateway of civilization in the series of Buland Darwaza. Patna 's landmark civilization gate has written the messages of four great men associated with Bihar. The voice of Mahatma Buddha, the originator of Buddhism, Lord Mahavir, the 24th Tirthankar of Jainism, Emperor Ashoka , Megasthenes is attracting tourists. CM Nitish Kumar inaugurated Civilization Gate on 20 May 2018 at Samrat Ashok Convention Center campus located near Gandhi Maidan.
