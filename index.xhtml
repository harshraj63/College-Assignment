Explore Patna

When we think about travelling in Patna city, what tourist spots strike in our mind?

  • The bank of Ganges
  • The Golghar
  • Takht Sri Patna Sahib
  • Patna Museum
  • Shri Krishna Science Center
  • Patna Zoo
  • The Planetarium

These places are worth travelling but nowadays these have become boring as one wouldn't want to go to same place again and again. People always want something new. But these are not the only places you can visit in Patna.

As the city is developing, it gained new points of intersets, new places to visit and so it gained attraction very quickly.

Though most of these are already well known by people, but some are still unaware.
