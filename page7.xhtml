7. BSPHCL Energy Park

Suggested duration :- 2-3 hours


Entry Charge:-


Park Entry :- 20₹
Yellow band(Boating) :- 60₹
Red band(Unlimited Activities) :- 120₹
Golden band(Unlimited All Activities) :- 200₹

Though this park is not as big as Eco Park, still it gives same amount of pleasure. This park is created and managed by BSPHCL. Park theme is based on Energy and Electricity. You can find different things related to energy and its discovery in the park. Also the park have lots of fun activities inside such as boating and Giant Swing.
