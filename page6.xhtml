6. Eco Park (Rajdhani Vatika)

Established :- October 2011



Stays open:- 08:00 AM - 08:00 PM
Stays closed:- Thursday
Entry Charge:- 20₹

Patna Eco Park is a large green area designed to provide a natural place for relaxation. There are over 3000 flora types, a children's playground, boating rides, jogging, and cycling trails, food court, natural theme decorations, and sometimes sculpture shows. The area has 1,445 metres of pathway, a children's corner and 1,191 metre jogging track. It has been developed to ease the load of Patna Zoo. The park is spread over 9.18 hectares of land, including two lakes. It is only spot in the busling city where the air feels fresh, and the green soothes the eyes for a while. With so much to offer, the Eco Park becomes one of the most loved tourist places in Patna.

● Skating section :-


There is a skating section where many people come and skate with skating shoes and skateboards.
