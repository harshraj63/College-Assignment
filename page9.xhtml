9. Marine Drive Patna

Suggested time :- evening

Patna Marine Drive is an under-construction expressway along the Ganga river in Patna, Bihar, India. It is expected to be completed by the end of 2022. It is a 21.5-kilometre-long (13.4 mi) road built between Digha and Deedarganj in Patna. It is planned as 4.5 km long riverfront along Ganges. 20 ghats were to be developed along a 2.3-km promenade with four cultural centres on the stretch of Patna riverfront. 4.5-metre wide esplanade has to stretch from Collectorate Ghat to Adalat Ghat (470m), Adalat Ghat to Gandhi Ghat (1132mt), Gandhi Ghat to Rani Ghat (384m) and — after a gap of few ghats — Bhadra Ghat to Nauzar Ghat (330m). It's near the sunset point, where people come to enjoy the evenings. This place gives full vibes of a beach.
