ISKCON Temple is a hindu relegious temple, situated in the heart of Patna which is the main temple of Patna. Founder-Acharya: His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada Visited Patna With First Governor of bihar and he has wrote later for preaching in bihar. His deciple HH Jayapataka Swami maharaj has started preaching in patna (bihar) and his dream was glorious temple in patna now srila prabhupad desire and HH Jayapataka Swami maharaj dream has fullfilled. Here you can enjoy the ecstatic Kirtan and prasadam throughout the day.

Location :-

Golok Dham, Budh Marg, Adalatganj, Veerchand Patel Road Area, Patna, Bihar 800001

Points of intersets :-

● Sandhaya Arati :-


The Sandhaya Arati here, which is scheduled on 07:00PM everyday, is a massive attraction here. Devotees visit at this time to enjoy the Arati. They worship, sing and dance together to show gratitude to lord Krishna. The scene is exotic at that time. Come to the right time and you won't go back without dancing and uttering "RADHE RADHE".

● Geeta Paath :-


Shrimad Bhagwat Geeta paath is organised here daily. If you're interested in Shrimad Bhagwat Geeta and its 'Shlokas', You're at the right place.

● Prasadam :-


In ISKCON Temple, after the Puja, you can get 'Prasadam' on the way out. 'Prasadam' is a term for offerings for lord Krishna. Usually its 'Khichdi' as prasadam.
